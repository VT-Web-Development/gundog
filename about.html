Hello From Marge and Rodger, Owners of Gun Dog Kennels

Gun Dog Kennels breeds and trains the ultimate gun dog companion, the German Shorthaired Pointer. Margery and Rodger have been training and hunting gun dogs since 1990. Now we would like to pass along the joy of hunting with a good dog, to you.

Our kennel offers puppies, started dogs when available, training, and boarding. We are located in Vermont's beautiful Northeast Kingdom, just a few miles from interstates 91 and 93.

Contact us today, and inquire about what we have to offer.

Our training philosophy

We train only pointing breeds, and limit the number of dogs to only a few per training season to assure your dogs gets the best personal attention possible.

Customer's dogs must be current with all shots. And dogs sent to us will be handled with the same care as our own fine hunting companions. We care about every dog. And please click on images for a larger view.
